Graphic Design

Graphic design is a profession, academic discipline and applied art whose activity consists in projecting visual communications intended to transmit specific messages to social groups, with specific objectives.
Usually, graphic design uses the aesthetics of typography and the compositional arrangement of the text, ornamentation, and imagery.
Current common examples are logos, banners and advertisement for companies and business.
