Digital Illustration

Digital painting/illustration mainly refers to the process of creating paintings on computer software based on computers or graphic tables.
Through pixel simulation, digital brushes in digital software
can imitate traditional painting paints and tools, such as oil, acrylic acid, pastel, charcoal, and airbrush.
