Value of Digital Art

The most common controversy surrounding digital art is it's value. The general critic is, because the media is digital (computer made), it's easy to produce and has no value.
Contradictory to this belief, digital art is not as easy as pressing one button. The different techniques always use the same basic tool; a drawing tablet, a mouse, or both.
Mastering both of these tools, and finding a technique that works for the final piece you wanna create, takes a lot of time, practice and study.
Compared to traditional art (Mistakenly referred as "hand drawn"), digital art requires the same level of muscular and visual memory.
