Artificial Inteligence Art

As technology develops, the artificial inteligence generated art became extremely popular.
Many critics proclaimed the demise of art, the elimination of creative jobs, and the erosion of human creativity.

Those critics, which are not invalid, are still arbitrary. The solid and big issue is, art theft.
Artificial inteligences (AI, for short) need to be trained using thousands of digital artwork, which is commonly stolen
and used without the artist's permission.
To start, reposting an artist's work without their permission is a copyright infringement.
By common sense (and copyright laws), the use of copyrighted work for any kind of project is also a copyright infringement.
Meaning that the training of AIs with other artist's works is, by simple math, art theft.
